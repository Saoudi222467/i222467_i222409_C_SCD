Hussaini

Hussaini is a remote village located in the Upper Hunza Valley of Gilgit-Baltistan, renowned for its iconic suspension bridge and breathtaking mountain scenery. With a population of fewer than 1,000 people, the village exudes tranquility and charm, offering a glimpse into traditional Wakhi culture and way of life. Visitors can cross the Hussaini Suspension Bridge, one of the most photogenic bridges in the world, and explore the village's rustic streets, ancient mosques, and terraced fields. Hussaini's serene ambiance and stunning natural beauty make it a must-visit destination for adventurers and photographers exploring Gilgit-Baltistan.
