Ratti Gali Lake

Rathi Gali Lake, a hidden gem in the rugged mountains of Azad Kashmir, lies at an elevation of 3,100 meters, surrounded by snow-capped peaks and verdant meadows. Its pristine waters reflect the azure sky, offering a mesmerizing sight for travelers. Popular among trekkers and nature lovers, the lake provides a serene retreat amidst the unspoiled wilderness. Renowned for its tranquility and natural beauty, Rathi Gali Lake promises an unforgettable experience amidst the majestic Himalayas.
