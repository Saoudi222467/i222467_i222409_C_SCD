Neelum Valley (Including Sharda, Kel, and Arang Kel)

Neelum Valley, a picturesque haven in Azad Kashmir, encompasses charming villages such as Sharda, Kel, and Arang Kel. Situated at elevations ranging from 1,600 meters to over 2,000 meters, the valley offers diverse terrain, from verdant meadows to towering peaks. Rich in cultural heritage, visitors can explore ancient temples, historical sites, and vibrant local markets. With its serene lakes, lush forests, and breathtaking vistas, Neelum Valley is a paradise for nature enthusiasts and adventure seekers alike.
