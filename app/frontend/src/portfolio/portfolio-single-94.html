Shogran Valley

Shogran Valley, situated at an altitude of 2,362 meters, captivates visitors with its enchanting beauty and refreshing climate. Blanketed by dense pine forests and embraced by snow-capped peaks, the valley exudes an aura of serenity and tranquility. Adventure enthusiasts can partake in hiking, horse riding, and camping amidst the pristine wilderness. Famous for its panoramic views and idyllic landscapes, Shogran Valley offers a rejuvenating escape from the chaos of urban life.
