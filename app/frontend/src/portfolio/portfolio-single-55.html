Moen Jo Daro

Moen Jo Daro, meaning "Mound of the Dead," is an ancient archaeological site located in the Sindh province of Pakistan. Dating back over 4,000 years, it was once a thriving city of the Indus Valley Civilization. Visitors can explore its well-preserved ruins, including the Great Bath, granaries, and residential structures, gaining insight into the daily life and culture of this ancient civilization.
