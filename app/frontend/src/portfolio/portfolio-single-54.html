Hyderabad

Hyderabad, a city steeped in history, is situated along the banks of the Indus River. Known for its vibrant bazaars, intricate handicrafts, and delicious cuisine, Hyderabad offers a glimpse into Sindhi culture and traditions. Visitors can explore its bustling markets, visit the Institute of Sindhology to learn about the region's history, or enjoy a leisurely stroll along the picturesque banks of the Indus River.
