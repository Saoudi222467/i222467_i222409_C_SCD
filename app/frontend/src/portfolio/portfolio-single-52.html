Karachi

Karachi, the bustling metropolis of Pakistan, is its largest city, known for its vibrant culture and diverse population. Situated on the Arabian Sea coast, Karachi boasts a rich history dating back centuries. It serves as the country's economic hub and is home to numerous tourist attractions, including museums, historical sites, and bustling markets. Visitors can explore the Karachi Museum to learn about the region's heritage or pay respects at Jinnah's Mausoleum, a symbol of the nation's founder.
