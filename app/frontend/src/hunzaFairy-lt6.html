Hunza & Fairy Meadows Expedition

11 Days - 10 Nights | A journey through Gilgit-Baltistan, Pakistan

Departure : Group Trips -

Fitness Level 3: 4-6 hours of physical activity per day | 3500-5000 ft above sea level.

Culture, History, Scenic Drives & Hiking

$2500-5000/person

Introduction

Venture on an unforgettable adventure through the breathtaking landscapes of northern Pakistan, beginning with your arrival in Islamabad. From the serene meadows of Naran to the awe-inspiring views of Nanga Parbat at Fairy Meadows, this journey promises scenic drives, treks, and cultural exploration. Discover the rich heritage of Gilgit and the ancient forts of Hunza, marvel at the beauty of Attabad Lake, and cross into the majestic heights of Khunjerab Pass. As the journey concludes, immerse yourself in the vibrant city of Islamabad before departing with memories of Pakistan's stunning natural beauty and cultural treasures.

Highlights

Trek to Fairy Meadows

Venture on an exhilarating trek to Fairy Meadows, nestled beneath Nanga Parbat. Along the way, encounter stunning landscapes and panoramic views, making it a must-do adventure for nature enthusiasts

Scenic Drive through Kaghan Valley

Traverse from Islamabad to Naran, soaking in the serene beauty of Kaghan Valley. Lush meadows and towering peaks create a picturesque backdrop for this tranquil journey, offering a rejuvenating escape

Cultural Exploration in Hunza

Dive into the rich heritage of Hunza Valley, home to ancient Silk Route remnants, historic forts, and bustling bazaars. Engage with locals, sample traditional cuisine, and absorb the vibrant atmosphere

Spectacular Views at Khunjerab Pass

Journey to Khunjerab Pass, the world's highest border crossing, for awe-inspiring vistas of snow-capped peaks. Explore quaint villages like Ghulkin and Passu, enhancing the allure of this mountainous region.

Diverse Landscapes on Return Journey

Experience the ever-changing scenery from Gilgit to Naran or Bisham. Each turn reveals new landscapes, from verdant valleys to cascading rivers, providing a captivating journey back.

Itinerary

Upon arrival in Islamabad, settle into your hotel for an overnight stay, allowing time to unwind and relax. Depart for Naran the next day. Enjoy a scenic drive via Balakot/Kiwai, witnessing lush meadows in Naran.

Adventure from Naran to Fairy Meadows (FM) via landmarks like Lulusar Lake, Babusar Pass. Adventure to FM requires 4WD off-road transport. Trek offers Nanga Parbat vistas. Following this, explore the city’s heritage and enjoy an overnight stay in Gilgit.

Discover Hunza's Silk Route, Rakaposhi viewpoint, Baltit and Altit forts, Karimabad Bazaar. Visit Hopper Nagar, Attabad Lake, and experience the local culture of Gojal valley.

Explore Ghulkin, Hussaini, Passu villages. Venture to Khunjerab Pass, the highest border crossing and return to Gilgit for an overnight stay.

On the trip back, admire diverse landscapes. Reach Naran or Bisham for an overnight stay, Return to Islamabad for an overnight stay. The following day enjoy a city Experience and then transfer to the airport for international departure

Who can join us?

This adventure is designed for travelers who seek to immerse themselves in the unparalleled beauty and diverse experiences of Northern Pakistan. Whether you're a thrill-seeker eager to trek the breathtaking trails of Fairy Meadows, a cultural enthusiast drawn to the vibrant heritage of Hunza, or a nature lover enchanted by the serene vistas of Kaghan Valley, there’s something here for everyone.
