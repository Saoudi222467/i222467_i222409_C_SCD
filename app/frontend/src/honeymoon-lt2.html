Enchanting Escapade: VIP Honeymoon Expedition

11 Days - 10 Nights | A Journey through Gilgit-Baltistan

Culture, Nature Discovery & Sightseeing

Introduction

Venture on an unforgettable journey through the stunning landscapes and rich history of northern Pakistan. From the moment you arrive in Islamabad, you'll be welcomed with warmth and comfort, setting the tone for an adventure filled with cultural exploration and natural wonders. Begin your trip with a guided Experience of Islamabad's iconic landmarks before heading to the breathtaking Skardu and its crystal-clear lakes. Experience the serene beauty of Fairy Meadows, trek through the majestic Hunza Valley, and marvel at the grandeur of Khunjerab Pass. Whether you're hiking through the scenic terrain, exploring ancient forts, or simply soaking in the panoramic views of Attabad Lake, this Experience offers an immersive blend of adventure, culture, and relaxation. Join us for a once-in-a-lifetime experience that showcases the best of Pakistan's natural beauty and historical significance.

Highlights

Scenic Karakoram Highway Drive

Traverse the legendary Karakoram Highway from Islamabad to Bisham, enjoying picturesque vistas and refreshing tea breaks amidst the grandeur of nature.

Heritage Exploration in Karimabad Hunza

Discover the historical depth of Karimabad Hunza at the Rakaposhi viewpoint, and explore the ancient Silk Route remnants and the majestic Baltit and Altit Forts.

Hunza Valley Adventure

Marvel at the vibrant floral tapestry of Hunza Valley, including the enchanting Hopper Nagar, Hopper Glacier, and the stunning Attabad Lake, all set against a backdrop of majestic mountains.

Cultural Immersion

Immerse yourself in the rich cultural heritage of Gulmit with visits to a traditional carpet weaving center and historic houses, surrounded by the blooming landscapes.

Spectacular Pak-China Border Journey

Venture on an awe-inspiring journey to the Pak-China border, taking in breathtaking views and exploring the historical remnants and vibrant bazaars along the ancient Silk Route.

Itinerary

Upon your arrival at Islamabad International Airport, our staff will warmly welcome you and assist with your transfer to the Pearl Continental Hotel Rawalpindi or Roomy Signature Islamabad for check-in. Spend the rest of the day relaxing and acclimatizing to your surroundings.

After breakfast, Venture on a guided tour of Islamabad. Visit the iconic Faisal Mosque, a masterpiece of modern Islamic architecture, followed by a trip to the Pakistan Monument, representing the nation's unity and diversity. Explore the Lok Virsa Museum to delve into Pakistan's rich cultural heritage, and enjoy panoramic views of Islamabad from Daman-e-Koh.

Transfer to the airport for your domestic flight to Skardu. Upon arrival, check-in at Shangrilla Resort Skardu. Later, explore Skardu's attractions including Upper and Lower Kachura Lakes, Satpara Lake, and the Skardu Cold Desert. Return to the resort for an overnight stay.

After breakfast, depart for Raikot Bridge. From there, take a 4WD ride to Tatoo village, followed by a scenic hike to Fairy Meadows. Along the way, admire the breathtaking views of Nanga Parbat. Check-in at Raikot Sarai or similar accommodation for an overnight stay.

Spend the day exploring Fairy Meadows at your leisure. Options include hiking to Nanga Parbat Base Camp, Biyal Camp, or the Nanga Parbat viewpoint. Alternatively, relax and soak in the stunning scenery. Return to Raikot Sarai or similar for another night's stay.

After breakfast, trek back to Tatoo village and continue by 4WD to Raikot Bridge. Depart for Karimabad in Hunza Valley. Enroute, visit the Junction Point of Mountain Ranges in Jaglot, remnants of the Ancient Silk Route, and the Rakaposhi viewpoint. Check-in at Eagles Nest or Hard Rock Hotel for overnight stay.

Explore Karimabad starting with Baltit Fort, an ancient fortress offering insights into Hunza's history. Visit Karimabad Bazaar for local handicrafts and cuisine. Later, visit Altit Fort, another historic landmark. Enjoy the evening at leisure in Karimabad. Overnight stay at Eagles Nest or Hard Rock Hotel.

After breakfast, depart for Attabad Lake. Enroute, stop at Altit Fort, Eagles Nest viewpoint, and Hopper Glacier viewpoint. Arrive at Luxus Hunza Attabad Lake or Famree Resort for check-in. Spend the evening admiring the scenic beauty of Attabad Lake.

Drive to Khunjerab Pass, the highest paved international border crossing, offering spectacular views of the Karakoram Range. Enroute, explore Passu village and Hussaini Bridge. Return to Luxus Hunza Attabad Lake or Famree Resort for overnight stay.

After breakfast, depart for Gilgit and continue to Bisham. Enroute, visit Taxila Museum and archeological sites. Arrive in Bisham for an overnight stay at Hilton Bisham (basic accommodation).

After breakfast, depart for Islamabad. Stop at Taxila for a museum Experience and archeological site visits. Proceed to Islamabad for airport transfer for your international departure.
