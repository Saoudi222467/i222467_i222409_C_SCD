Introduction

Join us at Crossroads Adventure on an exciting journey through Pakistan's history and culture! Begin in Karachi, where you'll explore famous places like Defence Mosque and Mohatta Palace. Then, Adventure from Thatta to Sukkur, discovering ancient sites like Chaukundi tombs and Shah Jahan Mosque. Next, visit Sahiwal's beautiful Bhong Mosque and Multan's shrines. In Lahore, see stunning UNESCO sites like Shalimar Garden and Lahore Fort. Finally, explore Islamabad's history, ending with a visit to Taxila. Join us for an unforgettable adventure!

Itinerary

Arrival in Karachi, followed by a local restaurant dinner and settling into your hotel. The next day, explore Karachi's iconic sites like Defence Mosque, Mohatta Palace, Jinnah Mausoleum, and St. Patrick's Cathedral.

Discover historic treasures en route from Karachi to Sukkur. Visit Chaukundi tombs, Banbhore ruins, and Shah Jahan Mosque in Thatta. The following day, explore Ranikot Fort, Sehwan Sharif, and the ancient marvel of Mohenjo Daro.

Engage with Bhong Mosque's intricate beauty, Derawar Fort's grandeur, and Uuch Sharif's spiritual sites. Journey onwards, visiting Multan's shrine-filled streets and Harappa's ancient archaeological site.

Adventure to Lahore, immersing in its UNESCO World Heritage sites: Shalimar Garden, Badshahi Mosque, and Lahore Fort. Dive deeper into history at Hiran Minar, Jangir's Tomb, and witness the Wagah border ceremony.

Adventure to Islamabad via Rohtas Fort and the iconic Shah Faisal Mosque. Discover Taxila's historical richness, the Dharmarajika Stupa, and finally, an airport transfer from Islamabad.
