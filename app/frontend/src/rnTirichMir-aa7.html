Tirich Mir Trekking Expedition

15 Days - 14 Nights | Unveiling the Majesty of Chitral, Pakistan

Departure : March - October | Private depending on bookings

Fitness Level 2: 6-8 hours of physical activity per day | 1500-12,467 ft ft above sea level.

Hiking and Trekking

$3000-5000/person

Introduction

Join us at Crossroads Adventure on an extraordinary journey to unlock the secrets of nature's grandeur with our 15-day Terich Mir trekking expedition in Pakistan. As the highest peak in the Hindukush mountain range, Terich Mir stands proudly at an astounding altitude of 7708 meters (25,289 feet), beckoning adventurers and explorers worldwide to witness its majestic beauty. Our meticulously crafted itinerary ensures an unforgettable experience, starting from Islamabad, traversing through the picturesque Lowari Pass, and arriving at the remote haven of Chitral.

Indulge in the organic experience of trekking on rocky paths, camping under the starlit sky, and savoring the local delicacies of Pakistan. With our expert guides and comprehensive services, including accommodation, meals, and transportation, your safety and comfort are our top priorities. Join us from May to September for an unforgettable adventure in the heart of Pakistan's wilderness. Don't miss your chance to elevate your soul and rejuvenate your spirit amidst nature's wonders.

Highlights

Remote Trekking Experience

Trek through the tranquil pastures of Bandok and Athak, leading to the Sheniak Birch Jungle, immersing yourself in the serenity of nature's untouched beauty.

Cultural Immersion with Kalash Tribe

Delve into the fascinating culture of the Kalash Tribe in the Kalash Valley, witnessing their unique customs, vibrant festivals, and warm hospitality.

Unparalleled Mountain Views

Feast your eyes on the awe-inspiring peaks of Tirich Mir, Istor o Nal, and Noshaq, adorned with breathtaking color contrasts, a visual treat for any nature enthusiast.

Glacier Concordia Exploration

Reach the pinnacle of your journey with a visit to Terich Concordia, where the Terich Glacier converges, offering a surreal experience amidst towering peaks and pristine glaciers.

Jeep Drive to Zani Pass

Experience the thrill of a scenic Jeep drive to Zani Pass, situated at an elevation of 3840 meters (12,598 feet), offering panoramic vistas of the Hindukush and Hindu Raj mountains, a perfect prelude to your trekking adventure.

Itinerary

Upon arrival at Islamabad International Airport, our team will warmly greet you and assist with your transfer to the hotel. After settling in, take a brief rest before Ventureing on an immersive Experience of Islamabad's iconic landmarks. Explore the majestic Faisal Mosque, immerse yourself in the cultural exhibits at Lok Virsa Museum, enjoy a serene stroll around Rawal Lake, and marvel at the grandeur of Pakistan Monument. Relax and unwind at the hotel after a day of exploration.

Start your day with an early breakfast at the hotel before beginning your journey to Chitral, a scenic valley nestled amidst the Hindukush mountains. The enchanting Chitral Valley is approximately 389.8 km away from Islamabad. Adventure via the breathtaking Lowari Pass, a mountainous route connecting Dir region to Chitral. Take in the awe-inspiring natural scenery along the way. Arrive in Chitral in the evening and check into your hotel for dinner and overnight stay.

After a delicious breakfast, kickstart your trekking adventure towards Shagrom, a picturesque valley located at an elevation of 2900 meters. Begin with a scenic drive to the magnificent Zani Pass viewpoint, situated at 3480 meters, offering panoramic vistas of the Hindukush and Hindu Raj mountains. From there, commence your trek downwards to Shagrom valley, the ultimate human settlement in the region. Arrive at Terichmir Hotel for a well-deserved rest and dinner.

Fuel up with a hearty breakfast before Ventureing on a scenic trek towards the Sheniak Base Camp at 3540 meters. Traverse alongside the Terich River, passing through terraced fields, lush grasslands adorned with fragrant wildflowers, and verdant summer pastures. After a pleasant 5 to 6 hours of trekking, arrive at the base camp where you'll spend the night under the starlit sky.

Start your day with a simple outdoor breakfast amidst the serene mountain surroundings. Continue your trekking journey towards Shughur Baisun, situated at an elevation of 4038 meters. Experience the awe-inspiring beauty of the lower Terich Glacier as you make your way to the campsite. After a tiresome but rewarding 5 to 6 hours of trekking, reach the camp area where you'll spend the night amidst nature's tranquility.

Rise and shine to a simple outdoor breakfast before Ventureing on the final ascent to Terich Mir, the highest peak in the Hindukush range. Trek alongside the grandeur of the upper Terich Mir Glacier and witness the magnificence of Terich Mir itself. After a perpetual experience of 5 to 6 hours of trekking, reach the Noshaq and Istor o Nal base camp at 4700 meters. Marvel at the panoramic views of Terich Concordia and surrounding peaks before camping beside a pristine mountain lake for the night.

Today is dedicated to leisure and exploration amidst the breathtaking natural beauty of the surroundings. Take in the serene ambiance of being on the roof of the world and enjoy the crystal-clear lake surrounded by lush grasslands and precious wild herbs. Spend the day exploring the Glacier Concordia and soaking in the unparalleled beauty of the landscape. Return to camp for dinner and overnight stay.

Begin your descent from the heights of Terich Mir towards Shughur Baisun. After a 4 to 5 hours trek, arrive at the campsite where you'll spend the night.

Continue your descent as you trek down to Sheniak, passing through picturesque birch and willow tree forests. Arrive at the campsite after 4 to 5 hours of trekking and spend the night under the starry sky.

Venture on the final leg of your trekking journey as you make your way back to Shagrom. After a 4 to 5 hours trek, reach your destination and spend the night in a local house, enjoying a delicious dinner.

Bid farewell to the mountains as you Venture on a scenic 5-hour jeep drive back to Chitral. Explore the vibrant city bazaar and immerse yourself in the local culture. Check into your hotel for dinner and overnight stay.

Venture on a cultural exploration of the Kalash Valley, renowned for its unique culture and traditions. Visit the sub-regions of Biri Valley, Bumburet Valley, and Rambur Valley, all inhabited by the Kalash people. Immerse yourself in the rich cultural heritage of the tribe before returning to Chitral for the night.

After lunch, board a flight to Peshawar, a city steeped in history and culture. Explore the bustling Qissa Khwani Bazar and visit the Peshawar Museum to witness the remains of the Gandhara Civilization. Enjoy dinner and spend the night in the city.

Venture on a scenic drive back to Islamabad via the Motorway. Explore the bustling streets of Rawalpindi, visit Raja Bazaar, and unwind at Ayub Park, the largest park in Pakistan. Check into your hotel in Islamabad for dinner and overnight stay.

After breakfast, our team will transfer you to Islamabad International Airport for your international departure. Bid farewell to Pakistan with cherished memories of your adventurous expedition to Terich Mir.
