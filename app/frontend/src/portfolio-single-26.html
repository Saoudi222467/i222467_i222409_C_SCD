Gulmit

Gulmit is a picturesque village located in the Gojal Valley of Gilgit-Baltistan, nestled amidst towering peaks and lush greenery. With a population of approximately 6,000 people, the village offers a tranquil retreat for travelers seeking to escape the hustle and bustle of city life. Visitors can explore the village's charming streets, visit local markets, and interact with friendly residents to learn about their unique way of life. Surrounded by breathtaking scenery and cultural attractions, Gulmit is a hidden gem waiting to be discovered by adventurous souls and nature lovers alike.
