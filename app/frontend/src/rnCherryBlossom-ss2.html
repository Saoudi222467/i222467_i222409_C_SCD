Luxurious Hunza Cherry Blossom Experience

12 Days - 11 Nights | A Journey through Gilgit-Baltistan

Departure : March 26th | April 1st | April 5th | April 10th

Fitness Level 2: 2-4 hours of physical activity per day | 1,770-9,800 ft above sea level.

Culture, Nature Discovery & Sightseeing

$2500-4000/person

Introduction

Set out on an extraordinary 14-day voyage through the mesmerizing landscapes of Gilgit-Baltistan, Pakistan, on our exclusive Hunza Cherry Blossom Experience. This meticulously crafted expedition is tailored for discerning travelers seeking a fusion of luxury, culture, and breathtaking natural beauty. Experience the splendor of the Karakoram Highway, delve into the rich heritage of Hunza Valley, and witness the vibrant cherry blossoms in full bloom. From the captivating historic forts of Karimabad to the enchanting vistas of Attabad Lake and the vibrant bazaars along the ancient Silk Route, this Experience promises an unparalleled adventure through one of the world's most scenic and culturally rich regions.

Highlights

Scenic Karakoram Highway Drive

Traverse the legendary Karakoram Highway from Islamabad to Bisham, enjoying picturesque vistas and refreshing tea breaks amidst the grandeur of nature.

Heritage Exploration in Karimabad Hunza

Discover the historical depth of Karimabad Hunza at the Rakaposhi viewpoint, and explore the ancient Silk Route remnants and the majestic Baltit and Altit Forts.

Hunza Valley Blossom Adventure

Marvel at the vibrant floral tapestry of Hunza Valley, including the enchanting Hopper Nagar, Hopper Glacier, and the stunning Attabad Lake, all set against a backdrop of majestic mountains.

Cultural Immersion in Gulmit

Immerse yourself in the rich cultural heritage of Gulmit with visits to a traditional carpet weaving center and historic houses, surrounded by the blooming landscapes.

Spectacular Pak-China Border Journey

Venture on an awe-inspiring journey to the Pak-China border, taking in breathtaking views and exploring the historical remnants and vibrant bazaars along the ancient Silk Route.

Itinerary

Welcome to Islamabad, the vibrant capital of Pakistan. Upon arrival, our dedicated team will transfer you to your luxurious hotel where you can unwind and relax. Enjoy an evening of leisure in one of Islamabad’s premier dining spots, savoring gourmet local cuisine. This first day is designed to set the tone for a sophisticated and comfortable journey ahead.

Board a domestic flight from Islamabad to Gilgit, experiencing a panoramic aerial view of Pakistan’s northern wonders. Should there be any flight cancellations, a scenic drive will take you through the enchanting landscapes of Bisham or Naran. Enjoy the serenity of the region before arriving in Gilgit for a comfortable overnight stay.

Venture on a day trip to the mesmerizing Naltar Valley. Renowned for its pristine beauty and adventure opportunities, Naltar offers stunning vistas and serene surroundings. Return to Gilgit for an overnight stay. If there are issues with flights, Adventure back from Bisham or Naran to Gilgit by road, enjoying the scenic views along the way.

Adventure from Gilgit to the picturesque Karimabad Hunza. En route, marvel at the panoramic views of the Karakoram Range and the lush green valleys. Upon arrival, settle into your elegant accommodation in Karimabad, setting the stage for your exploration of Hunza.

Immerse yourself in a full day of exploration in Hunza. Visit iconic sites including the historic Baltit Fort and Altit Fort, and enjoy the vibrant local market. Delight in the breathtaking landscapes and cultural richness of Hunza, with an emphasis on comfort and luxury throughout your journey.

Begin your day with a journey from Hunza to the stunning Hopper Glacier and the enchanting Attabad Lake. Experience the vibrant cherry blossoms in full bloom and the pristine natural beauty of these locations. Return to your luxurious accommodations at Attabad Lake for a relaxing overnight stay.

Adventure from Attabad Lake to the charming villages of Gulmit and Ghulkin, exploring their unique cultural heritage. Continue to the Hussaini Suspension Bridge, renowned for its thrilling height and stunning views. Finish the day at the serene Borith Lake before returning to Attabad Lake for an overnight stay.

Venture on a journey to the dramatic Khunjerab Pass, the highest point on the Karakoram Highway. Marvel at the stunning vistas and historical significance of this location. On your return, visit the iconic Passu Cones and enjoy an overnight stay in Passu, surrounded by majestic landscapes.

Adventure back from Passu to Gilgit, enjoying the picturesque scenery along the way. Check into your elegant hotel in Gilgit for a comfortable overnight stay, reflecting on the majestic beauty experienced during the Experience.

Depart from Gilgit and Adventure to Naran or Bisham. This journey offers more opportunities to enjoy the breathtaking scenery of Pakistan’s northern regions. Arrive at your hotel and relax in preparation for the final stretch of your journey.

Adventure from Naran or Bisham back to Islamabad. Arrive in Islamabad and check into your hotel. Enjoy a relaxing evening or revisit any key sites before your departure the next day.

After a final breakfast in Islamabad, you will be transferred to the airport for your international departure.
