Khaplu Fort

Perched atop a hill overlooking the town of Khaplu, Khaplu Fort is a magnificent architectural marvel that dates back to the 14th century. With its towering walls, ornate carvings, and stunning views of the surrounding landscape, the fort offers a glimpse into the region's storied past and strategic importance along the ancient Silk Road. Visitors can explore its well-preserved courtyards, ancient halls, and intricately decorated rooms, immersing themselves in the history and grandeur of this historic landmark in Gilgit-Baltistan.
