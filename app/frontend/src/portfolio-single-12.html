Khunjerab Pass

Situated at an awe-inspiring altitude of 15,000 feet, Khunjerab Pass is the highest paved international border crossing in the world. Connecting Pakistan with China, it offers panoramic views of snow-capped peaks, vast glaciers, and pristine landscapes. A gateway between two nations, Khunjerab Pass is a symbol of friendship and natural wonder, attracting travelers seeking epic mountain scenery and cross-border adventures.
