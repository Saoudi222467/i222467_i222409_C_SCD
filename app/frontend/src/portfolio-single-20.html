Rakaposhi Viewpoint

Rakaposhi Viewpoint offers captivating vistas of Rakaposhi, one of the most majestic peaks in the Karakoram Range. With an elevation of 7,788 meters, Rakaposhi dominates the skyline with its snow-capped summit. The viewpoint, situated near the town of Nagar, provides an ideal vantage point for admiring the towering beauty of this iconic mountain. Visitors can witness stunning sunrises and sunsets, capturing unforgettable moments amidst the breathtaking natural scenery of Gilgit-Baltistan.
