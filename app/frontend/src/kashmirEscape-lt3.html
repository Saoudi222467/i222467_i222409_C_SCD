Kashmir Escapade: Trekking Rathi Gali & Alpine Getaways

11 Days - 10 Nights | Trekking Rathi Gali & Alpine Getaways from Islamabad

Departure : Group Trips - | Private depending on bookings

Fitness Level 1: 3-5 hours of physical activity per day | 1500-3500 feet ft above sea level.

Trekking and Hiking

$2000-3500/person

Introduction

Traverse through picturesque villages, trek to breathtaking peaks, and unwind in luxurious accommodations amidst nature's grandeur. This journey is your gateway to unforgettable memories, cultural exploration, and unmatched tranquility. Whether you're trekking to Siri Paye Meadows, camping under the stars at Rathi Gali Lake, or exploring the lush landscapes of Nathia Gali, every moment promises to captivate and inspire.

Highlights

Tranquil Retreat in Nathia Gali

Relax amidst the serene ambiance of Cloud Forest Nathia Gali, exploring its beautiful landscapes before returning for a peaceful stay at The Cloud Forest or Alpine Resort.

Alpine Adventure at Rathi Gali Lake

Trekking from Arang Kel to Dowarian and onwards to Rathi Gali Lake, offering breathtaking views and camping under the starlit sky.

Enchanting Neelum Valley Drive

A picturesque journey from Islamabad with captivating stops at Nathia Gali, Murree, and Ayubia, culminating in a serene stay at PC Hotel Muzaffarabad.

Itinerary

Venture on a picturesque drive from Islamabad to Muzaffarabad, the gateway to the captivating Neelum Valley. Covering approximately 525 kilometers over 9-10 hours, enjoy enchanting stops at Nathia Gali, Murree's Mall Road, Ayubia, and Pir Chinasi, offering serene ambiance and panoramic views. PC Hotel Muzaffarabad welcomes you for an overnight stay.

Drive from Muzaffarabad to Karen village, tracing the Neelum River over 130 kilometers in about 4 hours. Pass through charming villages like Keran and Sharda, enjoying lush forests and crystal-clear streams. Stay at Mir Continental Hotel for a serene night.

Begin with a scenic drive to Sharda, then trek to Arang Kel, covering 8 kilometers in 3-4 hours. Enjoy breathtaking views along the moderately challenging trek. Stay at Wanderlust Chapter 1 or similar accommodation.

Continue trekking from Arang Kel to Dowarian (6 kilometers, 2-3 hours), then to Rathi Gali Lake (4 kilometers, 2-3 hours). Camp at Rathi Gali for a memorable night under the stars.

Trek from Rathi Gali Lake to Taobat, covering 12 kilometers in 5-6 hours. Enjoy the scenic beauty and limited horse riding options. Stay at PC Guest House & Resort.

Drive from Taobat to Kiwai, then a Jeep drive to Shoghran over 6-7 hours. Pine Park Hotel Shoghran offers a peaceful retreat amidst nature's tranquility.

Venture on a 6-kilometer trek to Siri Paye Meadows or opt for horse riding. Return to Pine Park Hotel Shoghran for relaxation.

Drive to Cloud Forest Nathia Gali for exploration and relaxation. Stay at The Cloud Forest or Alpine Resort.

Explore the beautiful landscapes and attractions of Nathia Gali before returning to your hotel for relaxation.

After breakfast, drive to Islamabad and check into Marriott Islamabad for a comfortable stay.

Check out from Marriott Islamabad and transfer to Islamabad International Airport for your international departure, concluding your memorable journey.
