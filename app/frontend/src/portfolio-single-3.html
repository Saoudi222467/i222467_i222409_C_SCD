Gojal Valley

Gojal Valley, situated at the northern tip of Pakistan, is a pristine paradise amidst the towering peaks of the Karakoram Range. With a population of around 50,000 people, the valley is home to diverse ethnic communities, including the Wakhi and Burusho people. Visitors can explore charming villages and trek through lush valleys, marveling at breathtaking glaciers like Passu and Batura. Gojal Valley offers a serene escape for nature lovers and adventure enthusiasts
