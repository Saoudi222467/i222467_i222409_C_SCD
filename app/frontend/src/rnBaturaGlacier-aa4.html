Batura Glacier Trekking Expedition

16 Days - 15 Nights | Unveiling the Majesty of Hunza, Pakistan

Departure : Group Trips - | Private depending on bookings

Fitness Level 2: 6-8 hours of physical activity per day | 1500-3500 ft above sea level.

Introduction

Join Crossroads Adventure on this remarkable 16-day Batura Trekking Expedition in Gojal Hunza, Pakistan. Venture into the heart of the Karakoram Range and immerse yourself in the stunning landscapes and vibrant culture of northern Pakistan. With Crossroads Adventure, you'll experience the thrill of trekking through rugged terrain, exploring hidden valleys, and engaging with local communities along the way. Our expert guides will lead you on an unforgettable journey filled with adventure, exploration, and cultural immersion. Don't miss this opportunity to discover one of the world's most breathtaking regions with Crossroads Adventure.

Highlights

Batura Glacier Trek

Set out on a thrilling journey across the seventh longest glacier beyond Antarctica, offering breathtaking vistas and icy panoramas.

Karakoram Highway Adventure

Venture on an exhilarating exploration of the iconic Karakoram Highway, revealing diverse landscapes and natural marvels that will astound adventurers.

Cultural Immersion

Immerse yourself in the vibrant heritage of Passu and Hunza Valley, with visits to ancient forts, bustling bazaars, and engaging interactions with local communities

Local Cuisine and Hospitality

Delight in delectable traditional dishes and experience the genuine hospitality of the locals along the trekking route, forging unforgettable memories for explorers worldwide. Note that the treks do not pass through forests.

Scenic Treks

Trek through verdant meadows, rugged terrain, and majestic landscapes, providing international trekkers with an intimate encounter with Gojal Hunza's natural splendor.

Itinerary

Upon your arrival, our Crossroads Adventure staff will receive you at the new Islamabad International airport and you will transfer to the hotel. After a brief rest, we will explore the beauty of Islamabad, we will be visiting Faisal Mosque, Lok Virsa, and Pakistan Monument.

Today we will take a scenic flight to Gilgit and in case of flight cancelation, we will depart for Chilas by road. It would be a life-changing journey via the 8th wonder of the world, the magnificent Karakorum Highway. Best opportunity to capture clicks for the future. We will be experiencing the grand fiver Indus on one side and lofty peaks on another side of the rock, and yes the amazing lush green valleys in their organic form on our way.

We will drive to Passu today which is the base for the Batura glacier trekking. In case of flight cancelation on day 2 we will drive from Chilas to Passu via Karakorum Highway. In between stopover for the majestic view of a meeting point of three mountain ranges, the Karakorum, Hindukush, and Himalayas, and further the Passu Cones view.

Today is our first of trek and after an avg 5 hrs trek, we shall reach Yunzben and camp there. Gather your stamina, and prepare yourself for a thorough excursion of nature by foot. On our way we will encounter the graveled path and large rocks but as we ascend we will be relaxing our senses in another shade of nature, the pastures, and Juniper trees.

Yet another beautiful day of trekking. It again will be an avg 5 hrs trek and we camp at Ujhoupirt. After having our first night outdoor under the shining stars and cool breeze coming from the Glacier, we will further proceed upwards for our next destination, after an approximate 6 hours walk we will finally reach Ujhouprit, and encamp there for the night stay.

Today is about Yashpirt village, a place with incredible views and reliable water. What else you need other than the mesmerizing fragrance of wild roses, the lush green Junipers, willows, and tamarisks. After you walk for 45 minutes, you will get the first impression of the first ice Floe of Batura Glacier at a specific place called Kush Bel. Further 45 minutes trek will take us to Yinj Gar Dur where a cleft in a distinguished rock allows a stream to pass through it. The next 30 minutes trek will lead us to the Yashpirt (3302m). we will camp among the pastures, and huts surrounded by mesmerizing Juniper Forest. The rest of the day will be dedicated to photography, and sightseeing.

An enchanted trek begins soon after early morning breakfast. We will cross the ablation valleys with Juniper, birches, and willows, after 15 to 20 minutes of walk, we will reach Piyakh Sheet which is 3345m high from sea level which is known for a distinctive Polar Growth. Further, we will cross Fatima il Sheet, enjoy fill memorable views while trekking along a side stream. We will be passing through the wooden doorway, Werthum Nala which runs parallel to the Batura Glacier before for 7.5km then disappears under the Glacier, the Kukhil which is the center of livestock pens, and hillside huts, Shilmin its gave the unique name after the presence of cheerful purple flowers, and finally will reach Guchesham contains meadows with livestock pens and huts. We will encamp in this place while enjoying the spectacular views of the Batura wall.

Rest Day, free to explore

Now, it's time to trek back downwards, we will follow the same route which was taken for ascending. Special care is required, as you might be traveling on steep paths which will be slippery as being part of Batura Glacier.

The same route will be taken while giving full time to photography, natural exploration, and viewing the marvels of nature. The trek will be much easier than it was when we were moving towards the altitude.

After following the earlier route, we will finally reach the most serene valley of Hunza-Nagar District, the calmed Passu Valley. There is much more to explore in this magnificent place. Surrounded by high peaks, traversed by freshwater streams, occupied by terraced fields and fruit gardens, the beautiful valley has a lot to present. We will be having a village walk, and dine with locals.

Now, it's time to explore the tourist hub of Gilgit- Baltistan, the enriched Hunza Valley. The capital of Nunza Nagar District is Karimabad (2435m).We will explore the Karimabad Bazar for a deep cultural exploration. The UNESCO'S enlisted Altit, and Baltit Fort, view the unparalleled Passu Peak, Ultra Peak, and the Kuksel Peak. On guest's demand we can arrange for Ultar Meadow track for halfway or less. Night stay at Hunza.

After breakfast, we will depart for Gilgit city, the capital of Gilgit- Baltistan Province which is at a distance of 59.4km from Hunza, via Karakorum Highway. We will be traveling alongside the Hunza river. Excursion to the old Gilgit Bazar, explore the riverside picnic spots, and the Yasuni Budha. Night stay in Gilgit.

flight to Islamabad, in case of flight cancelation depart for Bisham by road.

Free day at Islamabad and in case of flight cancelation on day 14, depart from Bisham to Islamabad by road.

After breakfast, we'll transfer you to Islamabad airport for your international departure, bidding farewell to Pakistan with cherished memories of your trekking expedition.
