South East Pakistan Expedition

9 Days - 8 Nights | Discovering the Treasures of Southeast Pakistan

Departure : Group Trips - 5th of Each Month | Private depending on bookings

Fitness Level 1: 2-4 hours of physical activity per day | 656-1,770 feet ft above sea level.

Culture and Heritage

$2500-4000/person

Introduction

Experience the allure of ancient culture and history on our 9-days Experience from Karachi to Islamabad, meticulously crafted by Crossroads Adventure. Dive deep into the heart of Southeast Pakistan, where UNESCO World Heritage Sites and centuries-old monuments await your exploration. From the architectural wonders of Sindh to the historical marvels of Punjab, this journey promises an enriching adventure filled with cultural insights and gastronomic delights.

Highlights

Explore UNESCO Heritage Sites

Visit ancient marvels like Mohenjo-Daro, Ranikot Fort, and Harrapa, and witness the wonders of past civilizations.

Discover Southeast Punjab's Culture

Uncover the rich history and vibrant traditions of Southeast Punjab on an immersive journey of exploration.

Enjoy Local Cuisine

Delight in the delicious flavors of Sindhi and Punjabi cuisines, sampling dishes that reflect the region's culinary diversity.

Itinerary

Arrive in Karachi. Hotel transfer for overnight stay

Depart from Karachi and drive to Hyderabad via Hala, stopping en-route to visit Chaukundi and Makli tombs, Shahjehan mosque, and Keenjhar Lake. Overnight stay in Hyderabad.

Explore Hyderabad in the morning before continuing your journey to Larkana via Sehwan Sharief, visiting the shrine of Lal Shahbaz Qalandar. Overnight stay in Larkana.

Visit Mohenjo-Daro in the morning, then drive to Sukkur, stopping en-route to visit a temple in Larkana. Overnight stay in Sukkur.

Spend the day exploring Sukkur, visiting Daraza Sharief, the Shrine of Sachal Sarmast, and other sights. Overnight stay in Sukkur.

Drive to Derawar fort, stopping en-route to visit the Blue Mosque at Bhong. Overnight stay at a local house in Derawar city.

Continue your journey to Uch Sharief, visiting the shrine of Shah Jalaluddin Surk and Jewindi Bibi. Drive to Multan and overnight stay there.

Experience Multan in the morning, paying respects to the tombs of Shah Rukne Alam and Ghous Bahauddin Zakaria. Continue driving to Lahore, with a brief stop at Harappa. Overnight stay in Lahore.

Explore Lahore with visits to Lahore fort, Badshahi mosque, Wazir Khan mosque, Anarkali bazaar, and Shalimar garden. Witness the flag ceremony at Wagah border before departing for international flights. for the above come up with who can join, services included and excluded

Who can join us?

Our Experience is perfect for history enthusiasts eager to explore Pakistan's rich cultural heritage, adventure seekers looking to uncover off-the-beaten-path destinations, and food lovers with a passion for authentic Sindhi and Punjabi cuisines.
