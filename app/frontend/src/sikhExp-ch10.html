Introduction

Step into a captivating journey through the heart of Pakistan's rich heritage and cultural tapestry with our Sikh Heritage and Cultural Expedition: Baisakhi Mela Experience Pakistan. This immersive experience offers a unique opportunity to witness the vibrant festivities of the Baisakhi Mela, celebrated annually in April, marking the harvest season and the Sikh New Year. Dating back centuries, Baisakhi holds profound religious significance, commemorating the founding of the Khalsa by Guru Gobind Singh in 1699. Delve into the spiritual ambiance of dedicated Gurdwaras across Pakistan, each steeped in history and architectural splendor. Explore Lahore's majestic architectural wonders, including the UNESCO World Heritage Sites of Lahore Fort and Badshahi Mosque, dating back to the Mughal era. Traverse the historical city of Hasan Abdal, where sacred sites like Gurdwara Panja Sahib and ancient archaeological treasures await discovery. Join us for an unforgettable expedition blending spiritual reverence, cultural immersion, and historical exploration.

Itinerary

Arrive in Lahore, the cultural capital of Pakistan. Begin your journey by visiting Gurdwara Dera Sahib, dedicated to Guru Arjan Dev Ji, near Lahore Fort. Explore the majestic Lahore Fort and Badshahi Mosque, iconic landmarks of Mughal architecture. Conclude the day with a delightful traditional Pakistani dinner at a local restaurant.

Venture on a comprehensive exploration of Lahore's historic Walled City. Visit the ornate Wazir Khan Mosque and delve into Lahore Museum's treasures. Pay homage at Gurdwara Janam Asthan, birthplace of Guru Ram Das Ji. Immerse yourself in the bustling ambiance of Anarkali Bazaar. Sample Lahore's renowned street food during a flavorful culinary Experience.

Set off for Kartarpur, the serene final abode of Guru Nanak Dev Ji. Experience the divine aura of Gurdwara Kartarpur Sahib and partake in the evening prayers. Relish the tranquility and spirituality that envelops Kartarpur. Enjoy an overnight stay in Kartarpur.

Journey to Hasan Abdal, home to the revered Gurdwara Panja Sahib. Explore the historic gurdwara, where Guru Nanak Dev Ji's handprint remains on a boulder. Discover nearby Sikh heritage sites, including Gurdwara Chakki Sahib and Gurdwara Sacha Sauda. Overnight stay in Hasan Abdal.

Depart for Rawalpindi/Islamabad, and visit Gurdwara Rori Sahib, associated with Guru Nanak Dev Ji's visit. Explore other Sikh heritage sites in Rawalpindi/Islamabad, such as Gurdwara Panja Sahib in Rawalpindi and Gurdwara Siri Guru Singh Sabha in Islamabad. Enjoy a farewell dinner at a local eatery.

Discover Islamabad's modern charm. Visit the majestic Faisal Mosque, a symbol of the city's architectural splendor. Delve into Pakistan's cultural diversity at Lok Virsa Museum. Relax amid nature at Lake View Park and Rawal Lake. Overnight stay in Islamabad.

Conclude your enriching journey through Pakistan's Sikh heritage. Transfer to Islamabad International Airport for your onward journey. Depart with cherished memories of the spiritual and cultural wonders encountered during your Experience.
