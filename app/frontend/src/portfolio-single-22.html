Baltit Fort

Baltit Fort, perched majestically above the town of Karimabad, is a UNESCO World Heritage Site and a symbol of Hunza's rich cultural heritage. Dating back over 700 years, the fort served as the residence of the Mirs of Hunza until the 20th century. Visitors can explore its labyrinthine corridors, ornate chambers, and ancient artifacts, gaining insight into the region's history and traditions. The fort's strategic location offers panoramic views of the surrounding valleys and peaks, making it a must-visit destination for history enthusiasts and adventurers alike.
