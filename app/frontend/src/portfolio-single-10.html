Besham

Bisham, a historic town in the Khyber Pakhtunkhwa province of Pakistan, boasts a rich cultural heritage and scenic beauty. With a population of approximately 45,000 people, Bisham is nestled in the picturesque surroundings of the Swat River Valley. Visitors can explore ancient archaeological sites, such as the Buddhist stupas and monasteries, which date back to the Gandhara civilization. Bisham also serves as a gateway to nearby tourist attractions, including the scenic Swat Valley and Malakand Pass, making it an ideal destination for cultural and nature enthusiasts alike
