Eagles’ Nest

Eagles’ Nest is a scenic viewpoint located near Karimabad, offering panoramic views of the Hunza Valley and surrounding peaks. Perched high above the valley, the viewpoint provides an ideal vantage point for capturing breathtaking vistas of snow-capped mountains, terraced fields, and traditional villages. Visitors can relax in cozy cottages nestled amidst lush gardens and enjoy the tranquility of the surroundings. Eagles’ Nest is a perfect retreat for nature lovers, photographers, and anyone seeking serenity amidst the majestic beauty of Gilgit-Baltistan.
