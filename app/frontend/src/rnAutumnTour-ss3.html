Skardu & Hunza Autumn Experience

11 Days - 10 Nights | Unveiling the Majesty of Gilgit-Baltistan, Pakistan

Departure : Group Trips on 15th of each month | Private depending on bookings

Fitness Level 2: 2-4 hours of physical activity per day | 1,770-9,800 ft above sea level.

Soft Adventure & Culture Exploration

$3000-5000/person

Introduction

Join us on an unforgettable journey through the breathtaking landscapes of Pakistan! Begin your adventure with a city Experience of Islamabad before heading to Skardu for a taste of its stunning scenery. In Khaplu and Shigar Valleys, soak in the beauty of ancient forts and mosques. Then, venture to Gilgit, Karimabad, and Attabad Lake, where you'll explore historic sites and enjoy scenic views. Experience village life in Gulmit and Passu before concluding your trip with a scenic drive back to Islamabad. Don't miss this chance to discover the natural wonders of Pakistan!

Itinerary

From the Islamabad International Airport enjoy a city Experience before transfer to your hotel where you will spend the night.

take a short domestic flight to Skardu. Explore the town, taking in the stunning landscape. Enjoy an overnight stay in Skardu.

A 3-hour ride brings you to Khaplu, where you’ll take in views of the magnificent valley and appreciate the history and heritage of the Khaplu fort, Chaqchan Mosque and local village. After a night of rest you’ll depart for Shigar Valley, traversing the Katpana Cold Dessert and enjoy the natural and historic sites of Shigar.

Journey towards Hunza on the adventurous Skardu Road. The long drive provides picturesque views of the valleys, charming villages, and terraced fields. Enjoy a Experience of Gilgit upon arrival with an overnight stay. The following morning, start towards Karimabad with stopovers at Rakaposhi Viewpoint, at the remains of the old Silk Road. Once in Karimabad take a late afternoon walk around the local market. After a night of rest, spend the entire day exploring the history and beauty of Hunza, with visits to the Baltit Fort, Altit Fort and Eagles’ Nest. Start the following day by visiting Hopper Nagar. After a break for lunch and herbal tea visit Attabad Lake, known for its turquoise blue waters and scenic backdrop. If desired, enjoy a boat ride.

Continue your journey to Gulmit, a picturesque village in Gojal Valley. Explore the village and interact with locals. Proceed to Passu, a scenic village offering panoramic views of the Passu Cones and the Batura Glacier. Take a short hike to experience the beauty of the area up close.

Enjoy the final leg of your journey, taking in the scenic beauty one last time. Several stopovers before an overnight in Naran allow for final photo ops. The following day, as you descend from the mountains, the landscape gradually changes to lush green valleys. Arrive in Islamabad and transfer to the airport for your departure flight.
