Heritage Trail: Islamabad - Lahore

4 Days - 3 Nights | Exploring Islamabad and Lahore's Cultural Treasures

Departure : Group Trips - | Private depending on bookings

Fitness Level 1: 3-4 hours of physical activity per day | 1500-3500 ft above sea level.

Culture and Heritage

$1500-3000/person

Introduction

Join us on an enchanting journey through the heart of Pakistan's cultural heritage with our "Heritage Trail" Experience, where you'll delve into the captivating cities of Islamabad and Lahore. Immerse yourself in the rich tapestry of history, architecture, and traditions as you uncover UNESCO World Heritage Sites, ancient ruins, and vibrant bazaars. From the serene beauty of Taxila to the majestic splendor of Lahore Fort, this Experience promises an unforgettable experience that will leave you captivated by the beauty and diversity of Pakistan's cultural treasures.

Highlights

Exploration of Taxila

Delve into the ancient ruins of Taxila, a UNESCO World Heritage Site, and discover the remnants of the Gandhara civilization. Explore archaeological wonders and delve into the rich history of this ancient city, accompanied by expert guides who will bring its stories to life.

Rohtas Fort Adventure

Traverse the rugged terrain to Rohtas Fort, another UNESCO World Heritage Site renowned for its historical significance and architectural brilliance. Explore the massive fortification, marvel at its strategic design, and uncover tales of valor and conquest that echo through its ancient walls.

Lahore Fort and Badshahi Mosque

Step back in time as you wander through Lahore Fort, a masterpiece of Mughal architecture, and behold the breathtaking beauty of Badshahi Mosque, one of the largest mosques in the world. Explore intricate marble carvings, stunning domes, and majestic courtyards that speak volumes of Lahore's grandeur.

Cultural Odyssey in Lahore's Old City

Lose yourself in the labyrinthine streets of Lahore's Old City, where history comes alive amidst bustling bazaars, ancient landmarks, and hidden treasures waiting to be discovered. Experience the vibrant colors, sounds, and flavors of Lahore as you explore its winding alleyways and immerse yourself in the city's timeless charm.

Itinerary

Upon arriving in Islamabad, you'll check in at the Envoy Continental or a similar hotel. After settling in, Venture on a captivating city Experience of Islamabad, starting with a visit to the majestic Faisal Mosque, one of the largest mosques in the world and an architectural wonder. Next, explore the Pakistan Monument, a symbol of the nation's unity. Enjoy a scenic drive through the city, passing iconic landmarks such as Daman-e-Koh and the Pakistan Museum of Natural History. After a day of exploration, return to the hotel for a restful overnight stay.

In the morning, check out from your hotel in Islamabad and depart for Taxila, a UNESCO World Heritage Site located about 35 kilometers away. Explore the ancient ruins of this important archaeological site, which dates back to the Gandhara civilization, and visit the Taxila Museum to view artifacts and relics unearthed from the area. Afterward, continue your journey to Rohtas Fort, another UNESCO World Heritage Site renowned for its historical significance and architectural splendor. Following your exploration of Rohtas Fort, drive to Lahore, taking in the scenic views along the way. Upon arrival in Lahore, check in at Hotel One or a similar hotel and spend the evening relaxing in preparation for the next day's adventures. Enjoy an overnight stay in Lahore.

After breakfast, set out on a full-day Experience to explore the cultural and historical gems of Lahore. Start with a visit to Lahore Fort (Shahi Qila), a UNESCO World Heritage Site, where you'll marvel at its magnificent architecture, including the Sheesh Mahal (Hall of Mirrors) and the grand Alamgiri Gate. Next, visit the Badshahi Mosque, one of the largest mosques in the world and an iconic masterpiece of Mughal architecture. Continue your journey at the Shalimar Gardens, another UNESCO World Heritage Site, known for its terraced gardens, elegant fountains, and picturesque pavilions. Dive into the lively atmosphere of Lahore’s Old City (Walled City), strolling through its narrow streets, vibrant bazaars, and historic landmarks. Conclude your day with a visit to the Wazir Khan Mosque, famed for its intricate tile work and vibrant colors. Return to the hotel in the evening to relax and unwind after a day of exploration, with an overnight stay in Lahore.

Start your day with breakfast at the hotel, followed by some time to relax or complete any last-minute shopping. Afterward, check out and transfer to Lahore Airport for your onward flight, bringing your unforgettable journey through Islamabad and Lahore to a close.
