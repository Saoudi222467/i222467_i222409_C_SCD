Hunza and Ghizer Valley Autumn Expedition

14 Days - 13 Nights | Unveiling the Majesty of Gilgit Baltistan, Pakistan

Departure : Oct - 5, 10, 15, 20

Fitness Level 2: 2-4 hours of physical activity per day | 1,770-9,800 ft above sea level.

Adventure & Culture

$2500-5000/person

Introduction

Join us for a 14-day luxury autumn expedition through the enchanting landscapes of Hunza and Ghizer Valley in Gilgit Baltistan, Pakistan. Tailored for high-end explorers, this journey combines adventure, culture, and nature, starting from the vibrant capital of Islamabad. Marvel at the stunning autumn foliage, delve into the history of ancient forts, and immerse yourself in the rich traditions of local communities. The itinerary features scenic drives along the iconic Karakoram Highway, cultural interactions in Gulmit and Ghulkin villages, and ample opportunities to photograph natural wonders such as Attabad Lake and the Passu Cones. Perfect for discerning travelers, adventure enthusiasts, and photographers, this exclusive expedition promises unforgettable memories and breathtaking experiences.

Highlights

Autumn Foliage in Hunza Valley

Witness the stunning transformation of Hunza Valley as vibrant autumn colors paint the landscape in hues of red, orange, and gold.

Historic Forts Exploration

Discover the rich cultural heritage of Baltit and Altit Forts, ancient landmarks surrounded by breathtaking scenery.

Scenic Karakoram Highway

Enjoy a luxurious drive along the iconic Karakoram Highway, with panoramic views of towering peaks, including Rakaposhi and the Passu Cones.

Local Cultural Immersion

Engage with the hospitable communities of Gulmit and Ghulkin villages, experiencing their unique traditions, cuisine, and warm hospitality.

Natural Wonders

Explore the pristine beauty of Attabad Lake, Hopper Glacier, and the mesmerizing Phandar Valley, perfect for high-end travelers seeking both adventure and tranquility.

Itinerary

Upon arrival in Islamabad, transfer to the hotel for an overnight stay, allowing time to unwind and relax.

Venture on a full-day drive along the majestic Karakoram Highway, passing through Abbottabad, Pattan, and Bisham. Enjoy dinner and overnight stay in Chilas.

Start with an early breakfast, then journey to Gilgit with a stopover. Continue the drive to Gahkuch, taking in the scenic beauty along the way. Dinner and overnight stay in Gahkuch.

After breakfast, head to Khalti Lake, then proceed to Phandar Valley. Enjoy free time for sightseeing and photography. Overnight stay in Phandar Valley.

Depart for Hunza Valley, stopping at Rakaposhi viewpoint and Nasirabad for photographs. Reach Karimabad, exploring remnants of the Old Silk Road and enjoying scenic views. Overnight stay in Karimabad.

Visit Baltit and Altit Forts, soak in the region's rich history. Explore the local bazaar, then drive to Nagar Valley, visiting Hopper village and glacier. Return to Hunza for dinner and overnight stay.

Enjoy breakfast before driving to Gulmit. Venture on a village walk from Gulmit to Kamaris and Ghulkin, experiencing the local culture. Dinner and overnight stay in Gulmit or Ghulkin.

Spend the day immersing yourself in the culture of Ghulkin village. Interact with a local family, enjoy village walks, and take in the scenic beauty. Overnight stay with the local family.

After breakfast, drive to Hussaini suspension bridge for a photo stop. Optionally, trek from Hussaini to Passu suspension bridge. Visit Passu Glacier and Cones before returning to Hunza for dinner and overnight stay.

Enjoy breakfast before Ventureing on a full-day drive to Bisham. Stop at Gilgit/Danyor, Chilas, and Pattan for sightseeing and refreshments. Dinner and overnight stay in Bisham.

Depart for Islamabad with stops at Mansehra, Abbottabad, and Taxila. Arrive in Islamabad, where you'll have free time at the hotel for relaxation. Overnight stay in Islamabad.

Early breakfast before departing for Peshawar. Explore Qissa Khwani Bazaar and Abdullah Khan Mosque. Return to Islamabad for an overnight stay.

Begin the day with breakfast followed by a city Experience. Visit Faisal Mosque, Pakistan Monument, Monal Restaurant, and Centaurus Mall. Return to the hotel for overnight stay.

Early morning pick-up from the hotel for transfer to Islamabad International Airport. Assistance with departure formalities for international flight.
