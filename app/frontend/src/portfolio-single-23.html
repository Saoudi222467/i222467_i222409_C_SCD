Altit Fort

Altit Fort, situated on a hilltop overlooking the Hunza Valley, is one of the oldest and most well-preserved forts in the region. Dating back over a thousand years, the fort offers a glimpse into Hunza's ancient history and cultural heritage. Visitors can marvel at its impressive architecture, intricate woodwork, and stunning views of the surrounding landscape. With its rich history and scenic beauty, Altit Fort is a captivating destination for those seeking to immerse themselves in the timeless charm of Gilgit-Baltistan.
